M3M and Jacob & Co Residences - Sector 97, Noida

Welcome to M3M and Jacob & Co Residences, a landmark ultra-luxury project in Sector 97, Noida, where modern architecture meets timeless elegance. This exceptional development is a collaboration between M3M India and the iconic luxury brand Jacob & Co., known worldwide for its craftsmanship and exclusivity.

Spread across 6 acres, the project features 4 majestic towers with only 250 exclusive residences, ensuring utmost privacy and space. Designed for those who value sophistication and comfort, the homes come in 3 BHK + SR, 4 BHK + SR, and 5 BHK + SR layouts, ranging from 2500 sq. ft. to 6400 sq. ft., with prices starting at ₹7.5 Cr and going up to ₹20.48 Cr.

Each residence is crafted to reflect modern luxury, featuring expansive interiors, premium finishes, and private spaces that redefine elegance. Residents can indulge in a world-class lifestyle with amenities like a grand clubhouse, infinity-edge swimming pool, gym, yoga & meditation zone, kids’ play area, indoor games lounge, mini theater, and lush landscaped gardens.

Strategically located along the Greater Noida Expressway, the project offers excellent connectivity to Amity University, Sikka Mall, Okhla Bird Sanctuary, and the upcoming Jewar Airport via the Yamuna Expressway. The 25:25:25:25 payment plan adds further convenience, making luxury living easier than ever.

With possession by November 2032, M3M Jacob & Co Residences Noida promises a life where design, comfort, and exclusivity come together to create a truly iconic address.

About M3M India & Jacob & Co

JACOB & CO. M3M Residences in Sector 97, Noida is a landmark luxury development crafted through a visionary collaboration between M3M India and iconic global luxury brand Jacob & Co. Sprawled over approximately 12.8 acres, the project offers a range of ultra-premium residences including 3, 4, and 5 BHK apartments and opulent penthouses. Each home reflects the signature style of Jacob & Co, known for bold design, precision, and opulence—mirrored here through sleek facades, expansive glass walls, oversized balconies, and panoramic city views. Residences come with imported Italian marble flooring, designer kitchens, smart home features, and spa-inspired bathrooms. The towers are designed for exclusivity with only 3–4 residences per floor, ensuring peace, privacy, and luxury. Residents can indulge in an array of 7-star amenities such as a rooftop infinity pool, clubhouse, spa, fitness center, mini theatre, fine dining, microbrewery, meditation gardens, sports zones, and concierge services. With biometric security, and a curated lifestyle experience, JACOB & CO. M3M is more than a residence—it is a testament to status, elegance, and world-class living in Noida’s most prestigious enclave.
