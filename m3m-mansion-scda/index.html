About SCDA

Welcome to Smart City Delhi Airport, a Master Development spanning approximately 100 hectares/247.105 acres. Envisioned as the epitome of urban living, it seamlessly integrates with the pulse of city life while embracing the serenity of nature. This visionary project aims to establish a sustainable and harmonious haven for its residents.
Within this urban utopia, residents discover a harmonious blend of luxury residences, workspaces, and recreational facilities amidst a bustling cityscape. Lush green streets, meticulously designed parks, abundant recreational areas, and architecturally innovative, energy-efficient buildings all converge to craft a skyline that harmonizes contemporary living with environmental consciousness.
The Master Plan of Smart City Delhi Airport is the brainchild of a select team of world-class architects. Their expertise and extensive research into global urban development inform the project's DNA. The focus of this master plan lies in creating a SMART infrastructure, characterized by pedestrian and cyclist-friendly roads, extensive greenery, improved air quality, diverse community spaces catering to all age groups, and dedicated recreational zones.
